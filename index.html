Technology

All-Wheel Torque Vectoring

Four independent motors provide precise torque control to each wheel, enabling unprecedented handling dynamics and traction in all conditions. The system calculates optimal power distribution 1,000 times per second.

Advanced artificial intelligence analyzes your driving patterns and provides real-time coaching to help you extract maximum performance from the Nevera while maintaining safety at all times.

Carbon Fiber Monocoque

Lightweight yet incredibly strong carbon fiber construction provides exceptional rigidity while keeping weight to a minimum. Every component is engineered for optimal strength-to-weight ratio.
